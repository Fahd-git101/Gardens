Gardening Advice

April Checklist

Prepare soil – remove weeds and add compost.
Plant summer bulbs – dahlias, lilies, gladioli.
Water regularly – check soil moisture daily.
Protect young plants – use cloches or fleece.
Feed your plants – apply balanced fertiliser.

Greenhouse Ventilation

Ventilate on warm days. Water in the morning to prevent disease. Use shade cloth during hot weather to protect delicate plants.

Pruning & Mulching

Prune shrubs after flowering. Mulch beds to retain moisture and suppress weeds. Check for pests and diseases weekly.

Allotment Jobs

Prepare beds for planting. Sow carrots, peas, and beans. Protect crops from birds with netting or scarecrows. (Allotment = a small plot of land rented for growing vegetables)

May Checklist

Harden off young plants before planting outside.
Plant out tomatoes, peppers, and cucumbers.
Water regularly and feed weekly.
Mow lawns more frequently.
Deadhead flowers to encourage more blooms.

Shading & Watering

Apply greenhouse shading to prevent overheating. Water early in the morning or late evening to reduce evaporation. Install automatic vent openers for better climate control.
